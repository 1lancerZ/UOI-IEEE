Annual Hardware Design Competition

April 20-22, 2025
Engineering Lab, Building 3

Event Description

This annual competition brings together the brightest minds in hardware design across the university. Participants will have the opportunity to showcase their innovative projects to a panel of industry experts and academic leaders.

Competition Structure

  • Preliminary Round: April 20 - Project submission
  • Semi-finals: April 21 - Prototype demonstration
  • Final Round: April 22 - Live presentation

Prizes

Winners will receive:

  • 1st Prize: $5,000 + Summer internship at TechCorp
  • 2nd Prize: $3,000 + Professional development package
  • 3rd Prize: $1,500 + Hardware toolkit
